## Excluded categories

### Livestock

Livestock has different production cycles, capital requirements, feed interactions, biological constraints, cost structures, and market mechanisms. Paper 1 focuses on arable crop portfolio allocation.

### Sugar Beet (sugar_beet)

Sugar beet has contract-based pricing and processor-specific economics. Including it would shift the paper toward contract-market analysis and weaken the clean comparison of market-priced arable crops.

### Maize (maize)

Maize has heterogeneous end uses (grain, forage, energy maize) with different economic mechanisms and is difficult to harmonize in Paper 1.
