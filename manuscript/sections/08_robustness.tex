\section{Robustness checks}
\label{sec:robustness}

Robustness dimensions follow charter \S16: historical windows, crop-universe variants,
cooperative scales, price assumptions, risk-aversion parameters, scenario-generation methods,
covariance estimators, and cost/yield fallback assumptions where implemented.
