\section{Data}
\label{sec:data}

\subsection{Crop universe}

The core crop universe is selected empirically from UK arable area shares over 20-, 10-, and
5-year windows using a Pareto cumulative-share procedure documented in the pipeline report.
Included crops are wheat, barley, oats, oilseed rape, potatoes, and peas/field beans.
Livestock, sugar beet, and maize are excluded with documented rationales in
\texttt{configs/assumptions.yaml} and the crop-universe report.

\subsection{Profit panel}

County--crop--season profits follow
$\text{profit} = \text{yield} \times \text{price} - \text{cost}$
with harmonized units and inflation adjustment where configured.
Imputations and fallbacks are logged; no silent imputation is permitted.
