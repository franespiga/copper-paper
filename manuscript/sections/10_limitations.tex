\section{Limitations}
\label{sec:limitations}

This section summarizes scientifically relevant limitations tracked in
\texttt{paper/MANUSCRIPT\_LIMITATIONS.md}; the full integration log is maintained separately.

\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Yield coverage.} County-level yield series rely on reference tables and
    documented national fallbacks where county data are unavailable.
  \item \textbf{Cost aggregation.} Enterprise gross margins and crop-level average variable
    costs may not capture farm-specific heterogeneity.
  \item \textbf{Price proxies.} Crop-season prices use published reference series; potato and
    minor-crop proxies may misstate local market conditions.
  \item \textbf{Covariance instability.} Sample covariance estimates for many county--crop
    assets are noisy; shrinkage and robust estimators are reported but uncertainty remains.
  \item \textbf{Cooperative adoption.} The model evaluates counterfactual allocations and
    sharing rules, not transaction costs, governance, or compliance with cooperative law.
  \item \textbf{UK external validity.} Institutional and agronomic conclusions may not
    transfer to other countries or to livestock and contract crops excluded from Paper~1.
  \item \textbf{Journal template.} Draft builds use an \texttt{article.cls} fallback until a
    frozen target journal and official publisher template are confirmed.
\end{itemize}
