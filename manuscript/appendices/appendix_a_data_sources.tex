\section{Data sources and provenance}
\label{app:data-sources}

Primary data categories include UK crop area and yield statistics (DEFRA / GOV.UK),
crop prices (AHDB and documented proxies), enterprise costs (Farm Business Survey and
reference margin tables), and spatial county definitions.

Full source URLs, access dates, file hashes, and processing scripts are listed in
\texttt{data/metadata/sources.csv} and \texttt{outputs/reports/data\_provenance.md}.
The synced supplementary copy is \texttt{supplementary/data\_provenance.md} in the
submission package.

Crop inclusion and exclusion rationales appear in \texttt{configs/assumptions.yaml} and
\texttt{supplementary/crop\_universe\_selection.md}.
