\section{Algorithmic details}
\label{app:algorithms}

Portfolio problems use \texttt{scipy} and \texttt{cvxpy} solvers with logged failures.
CVaR optimization follows a Rockafellar--Uryasev formulation; failures on small samples are
logged and non-fatal.

Bootstrap resampling defaults to dependence-preserving draws that retain cross-crop and
cross-county correlation within sampled years.

Experiment IDs, configuration paths, and output locations are listed in
\texttt{experiments/experiment\_registry.yaml} (synced to supplementary materials).
